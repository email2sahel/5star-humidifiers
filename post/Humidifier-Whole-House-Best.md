    <p>Yes, if not cleaned regularly. Use distilled water and <a href="https://5starchoices.com/do-humidifiers-cause-mold/">follow maintenance tips</a>.</p>
    <h3>Where should I place a whole-house humidifier?</h3>
    <p>Elevated, central spots work best. Avoid walls for even distribution (see <a href="https://5starchoices.com/where-to-place-humidifier/">placement guide</a>).</p>
    <h3>Do humidifiers require filtered water?</h3>
    <p>Recommended to prevent mineral buildup. <a href="https://5starchoices.com/can-you-use-filtered-water-in-a-humidifier/">Filtered or distilled</a> extends device life.</p>
    <h3>How long until a humidifier affects room humidity?</h3>
    <p>Within 1-2 hours for midsize rooms. Larger spaces may take 3-4 hours.</p>
